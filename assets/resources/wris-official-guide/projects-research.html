Publication & Sharing Policy

WRIS maintains a structured approach to sharing information. Internal communications, including draft reports, preliminary findings, and ongoing discussions, remain within secure channels to protect intellectual property and ensure accuracy. External publications, presentations, or public-facing outputs require review and approval by the executive council. Authorship is credited according to contribution, and members are encouraged to acknowledge collaborators appropriately. This policy ensures transparency, recognition, and compliance with professional and academic standards while promoting the responsible dissemination of knowledge generated through WRIS initiatives.
