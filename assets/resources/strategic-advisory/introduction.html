Introduction

In markets that shift with new technology, regulatory pressure, and evolving stakeholder expectations, professionals cannot depend only on instinct. The gap between instinct and insight often determines whether a transformation succeeds or fails. For auditors, advisory leaders, and those guiding organizational change, decisions must be grounded in data and evidence rather than intuition alone.
